Rice Milling

Advanced Technology for Premium Quality

One-Pass vs Modern Milling

Steel Single-Pass Mill (Traditional)

Traditional single-pass mills apply high pressure to remove husk and polish rice in one operation. This results in:

  • High grain breakage
  • Lower rice recovery
  • Reduced head rice yield

Due to these limitations, Eastern Rice does not rely on outdated systems for commercial production.

Two-Stage Milling

Compact Rice Mill

Two-stage milling separates hulling and polishing into two controlled steps:

  • Rubber rollers gently remove the husk
  • Polishing machines whiten the rice

This method significantly improves grain quality and recovery compared to single-pass milling, ensuring a better product for our customers.

Our Core System

Multiple-Pass Commercial Milling

Our advanced milling process is rigorous and thorough:

  1. Pre-cleaning of paddy to remove straw, stones, dust, and impurities
  2. De-husking using efficient hulling systems
  3. Polishing and whitening to remove bran layers as required
  4. Grading and separation of broken grains from whole kernels
  5. Hygienic packaging and bagging
  6. By-product management including bran and husk

Pre-Cleaning

A Critical First Step

Incoming paddy often contains foreign materials such as straw, weed seeds, soil, sand, and dust. Our pre-cleaning systems ensure:

  • Higher milling efficiency
  • Better rice recovery
  • Reduced machine wear

We use advanced combinations of oscillating sieve cleaners to guarantee purity from the start.
