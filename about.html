About Us

Our Journey of Growth and Excellence

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Eastern Rice Products (Private) Limited was established with a clear vision: to deliver high-quality rice while supporting Sri Lankan farmers and strengthening the local agricultural economy.

We began our journey with only four employees, operating with limited resources, a paddy yard, and traditional sunlight drying methods.

During our early days, we faced many challenges—financial limitations, operational difficulties, and supply constraints—but our commitment and perseverance kept us moving forward.

Through dedication, discipline, and trust, we gradually expanded our operations and adopted modern milling technology. Today, Eastern Rice operates with advanced machinery and structured processes, while staying true to the values that built our foundation.

Our Capacity & Strength

  • • We maintain bulk paddy stock exceeding 10,000 metric tons throughout the year
  • • All major rice varieties are stored and managed efficiently
  • • We operate across approximately 200 acres of locally spread spaces, supporting storage, processing, and logistics

This scale allows us to meet both seasonal and continuous demand without compromising quality.
