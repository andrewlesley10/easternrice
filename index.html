We are recognised as one of the largest rice mills in the Eastern Province, delivering a wide range of premium-grade rice products to local and island-wide markets.

Our Story

Quality You Can Trust

We accept only the best quality raw paddy harvested in Sri Lanka and process it using the latest milling technology available island-wide.

Our advanced systems ensure high quality, consistency, hygiene, and minimal grain breakage—meeting the expectations of modern consumers and bulk buyers alike.

With strong farmer partnerships, modern infrastructure, and year-round stock availability, Eastern Rice continues to serve households, retailers, wholesalers, and institutional buyers with trust and reliability.
